How to play:
  1. Make an account or log into a pre-existing account.
  2. Click the 'plus' icon in the nav bar to go to create a game
  3. Next, select game options:
  4. Total players (including you)
  5. Please note solo players cannot win or lose a game, but can earn points for questions right and wrong
  6. Category
  7. Difficulty
  8. Type of Question (true/false or multiple choice)
  9. The other players joining you (if multi-player)

The game will alternate players on each question and at the end of the game the scores are tallied

You can then view your stats by clicking on the account details icon on the nav bar.
